## 1. Executive Summary

**CloudMultiPloy** is a production-grade Internal Developer Platform (IDP) designed to reduce operational overhead for product engineering teams by providing self-service infrastructure provisioning, golden path templates, an integrated ticketing system, and deep integration with Azure cloud services (with future extensibility to AWS and GCP).

This project is developed following a realistic Scrum-based Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC). Every artifact — from requirements gathering and sprint planning to architecture decision records, infrastructure-as-code, application code, CI/CD pipelines, monitoring dashboards, and post-mortem templates — is committed to GitHub, demonstrating the full spectrum of Platform/DevOps engineering.

The platform is built with Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles at its core: service-level objectives (SLOs), error budgets, toil reduction, and progressive rollout strategies are a primary focus since the beginning of the project.
