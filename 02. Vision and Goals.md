## 2. Vision & Goals

### 2.1 Product Vision

> Empower every engineering team to ship production-ready software in minutes, not days, by abstracting infrastructure complexity behind self-service workflows and opinionated golden paths — without sacrificing security, compliance, or observability.

### 2.2 Strategic Goals

1. **Reduce Time-to-Production**: New services go from idea to deployed-in-production within a single sprint, not weeks.
2. **Eliminate Ops Toil for Dev Teams**: Developers never need to write Terraform, configure Kubernetes manifests, or set up monitoring from scratch.
3. **Enforce Organizational Standards**: Security policies, observability, cost controls, and compliance are baked into every golden path.
4. **Provide Radical Transparency**: Every infrastructure change is auditable, every deployment is traceable, and every service has a clear owner.
5. **Scale Horizontally**: The platform supports 10 teams today and 100 teams tomorrow without architectural redesign.

### 2.3 Success Metrics (OKRs)

| Objective | Key Result | Target |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Accelerate delivery | Time from service creation to first production deployment | < 30 minutes |
| Reduce toil | % of infra tasks handled via self-service | > 85% |
| Platform reliability | CloudMultiPloy platform uptime | 99.95% (SLO) |
| Developer satisfaction | Quarterly NPS from platform users | > 50 |
| Incident response | Mean time to detect (MTTD) for platform issues | < 5 minutes |
